Welcome to this website about 1940's and early 1950's Custom cars of Southern California

This website is designed to provide some insight to the type of cars that were built in 1946 - 1953 by Southern Californain Custom Car builders. Such builders opened their shops after WW II, and many these service men began redesigning their cars into Customs or Hotrods.
